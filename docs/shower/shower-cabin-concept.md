# Shower Cabin Concept

## Space

Approximate available shower cabin space: 700 x 700 mm.

## Preferred Construction

The target is a more premium-looking shower cabin than a standard one-piece white plastic camper bathroom module.

Preferred architecture:

- separate lightweight shower tray at the bottom
- lightweight waterproof wall panels
- compact shower door system

## Shower Tray Direction

Potential tray sizes:

- approximately 680 x 680 mm
- approximately 665 x 665 mm
- custom tray if required

Candidate tray types:

- ABS
- thermoformed plastic
- GRP / fiberglass custom tray

## Wall Panels

Desired properties:

- lightweight
- waterproof
- visually suitable for premium interior
- suitable for compact vehicle wet-room use
- easy to cut and install
- durable under vibration and temperature changes

Potential materials / suppliers:

- Senoplast / senosan caravan interior panels
- Targwall composite PVC wall panels
- Roth Vipanel
- Fibo
- RENOLIT lightweight caravan products

## Door / Closure

Preferred direction:

- tambour / roller door
- compact folding door

Avoid heavy domestic glass shower doors unless specifically engineered for vehicle use.
