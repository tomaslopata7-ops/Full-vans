# Shower Door Research

## Target

Find a compact shower door / closure solution for a premium camper van shower cabin.

Available shower cabin space: approximately 700 x 700 mm.

Preferred direction:

- space-saving opening
- low weight
- suitable for vehicle movement and vibration
- good sealing for shower use
- visually suitable for a premium camper interior
- ideally compatible with custom wall panels and separate shower tray

## Main Door Types

### Tambour / roller shower door

Why it is interesting:

- very space-saving
- no swing clearance required
- commonly used in campervan and motorhome wet rooms
- can follow an L-shaped track around the corner
- usually lightweight plastic / aluminium profile construction

Risks / open items:

- track and corner detail must drain / clean well
- visual quality varies a lot between suppliers
- sealing detail needs to be checked carefully
- needs a secure latch for driving

Current recommendation:

- strongest first prototype direction
- likely the best compromise for compact 700 x 700 mm cabin

### Retractable fabric / screen shower door

Why it is interesting:

- very compact cassette
- lightweight
- can be made to measure
- more refined than a loose curtain if executed well

Risks / open items:

- premium feel needs to be checked in person
- long-term cleaning, drying and mildew risk
- water containment depends heavily on bottom and side sealing detail

Current recommendation:

- worth evaluating as an alternative to tambour

### Bi-fold shower door

Why it is interesting:

- common domestic shower solution
- 700 mm products are widely available
- visually clean if using glass

Risks / open items:

- glass is heavy
- not designed for vehicle vibration unless specifically validated
- needs travel latch / locking
- hinge and seal durability in a moving vehicle needs validation

Current recommendation:

- not first choice for prototype unless using a lightweight plastic or vehicle-specific product

### Shower curtain / soft enclosure

Why it is interesting:

- lightest and cheapest
- easy prototype fallback
- no complex door structure

Risks / open items:

- not premium
- poor user experience in a compact shower
- can cling to user
- harder to dry and keep tidy

Current recommendation:

- use only as temporary prototype fallback

## Current Recommendation

For the first prototype, evaluate:

1. Reimo / Carbest shower tambour door set as the EU-first option.
2. Obelink / MonsterShop roller shower door as a quick retail prototype option.
3. Tambour / roller shower door from UK suppliers only if the EU options do not meet size or finish requirements.
4. Custom retractable waterproof door from a camper-specific supplier.
5. Vehicle-specific bi-fold door only if tambour/retractable options do not meet the desired premium feel.

Avoid domestic glass shower doors as the first direction because of weight, vibration, travel locking and impact-risk concerns.
