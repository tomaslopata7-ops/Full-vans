# Shower Wall Panels Research

## Target

Find lightweight waterproof wall panels for a compact premium camper van shower cabin.

Available space: approximately 700 x 700 mm.

Preferred direction:

- waterproof wall panel system or waterproof lightweight panel material
- visually suitable for a premium interior
- low weight and suitable for vehicle installation
- robust sealing / corner detail
- suitable for prototype and small-series production

## Current Shortlist

### RENOLIT

Website: https://www.renolit.com/en/interior-surfaces/renolit-leichtbauprodukte-caravan

Why it is interesting:

- caravan-specific lightweight products
- stated suitability for wet cells
- waterproof, decorative and lightweight product direction
- potentially stronger match for vehicle use than domestic bathroom panels

Contact direction:

- For Germany, Italy, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Finland, Hungary and Scandinavia:
  Matthias von Kleinsorgen, Sales Director
  matthias.von-kleinsorgen@renolit.com

Potential role:

- priority supplier for production-quality lightweight shower wall material

### Senoplast / senosan

Website: https://www.senoplast.com/en-mx/applications/caravans-motorhomes/

Why it is interesting:

- plastic sheets for caravan and motorhome interiors
- focus on lightweight surfaces, scratch resistance and premium finishes
- likely suitable for custom panel integration if the correct sheet type is selected

Potential role:

- priority supplier for premium plastic sheet / surface material evaluation

### Roth Vipanel

Website: https://www.roth-czech.cz/vipanel---wall-panels

Why it is interesting:

- lightweight aluminium composite wall panels
- designed for shower and bathroom wall renovation
- decorative printed surfaces

Open concern:

- needs confirmation for vehicle vibration, thermal cycling and small-series camper use

Potential role:

- good reference or prototype option if weight and installation details are acceptable

### Fibo

Website: https://fibosystem.com/

Why it is interesting:

- waterproof wall system for wet rooms and shower areas
- mechanical joint system and profiles may simplify sealing
- broad decorative range

Open concern:

- timber-based panel construction may be heavier than caravan-specific lightweight products
- needs confirmation for vehicle use and vibration

Potential role:

- backup / benchmark solution for waterproof wet-room panel system

### Targwall

Website: https://targwall.co.uk/home-2/

Why it is interesting:

- PVC wall cladding for shower cubicles and wet-room use
- visually decorative surface options
- easy installation concept

Open concern:

- needs confirmation for vehicle environment, temperature range and premium surface quality

Potential role:

- prototype or visual mockup option if lightweight and durable enough

## Recommended Outreach Order

1. RENOLIT - strongest caravan / lightweight wet-cell fit.
2. Senoplast - strong vehicle interior plastic sheet fit.
3. Roth Vipanel - promising bathroom composite panel, needs vehicle validation.
4. Fibo - robust wet-room benchmark, likely heavier.
5. Targwall - useful low-complexity PVC panel option, likely prototype/reference only.
